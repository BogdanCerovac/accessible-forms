VoiceOver test 01 - without ARIA

Return to main list

This test case is designed to determine how landmark elements are displayed in VoiceOver's Rotor Landmark list.

Example

1. Header content

2. Main content

3. Aside content

4. Nav content

5. Article content

6. Footer content

Results

HTML element Announced Chrome Rotor Firefox Rotor Sarafi Rotor
<header role="banner"> "Banner", "End of banner" Not displayed "banner" "banner"
<"main"> "Main", "End of main" "main" "main" "main"
<aside> "Complementary", "End of complementary" "complementary" "complementary" "complementary"
<nav> "Navigation", "End of navigation" "navigation" "navigation" "navigation"
<article> "Article", "End of article" "article" "article" Not displayed
<footer role="contentinfo"> "Footer", "End of footer" Not displayed Not displayed "contentinfo"
